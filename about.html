I graduated from the University of Georgia with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Fabric Design and a Master of Science in Textiles, Merchandising and Interiors focusing on Sustainable Fashion. My research investigated the social, environmental and economic considerations that can be made during the design process of sustainable fashion products. I am interested in how people and the environment are impacted through the production and consumption of textile goods and how designers can lead the way to a sustainable fashion industry.

Many of my textile designs are inspired by nature. I love discovering unexpected shapes and motifs in natural objects and reinterpreting them into my patterns. I enjoy working with watercolor and acrylic paints, creating natural textures and shapes through brush strokes, stamping, dotting and blending.
